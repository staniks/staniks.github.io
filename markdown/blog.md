## Worship Development Series

**Worship** is a small first-person shooter game I've been developing in my spare time for fun. While it was originally intended as a sequel to my [parody raycaster](inferno/index.html), I have decided to decouple the games in order to explore different avenues of creativity, while keeping most of the finished game assets.

This short blog series is designed to summarize some of my thought process and parts of development I found interesting.
